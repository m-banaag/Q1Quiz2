Kung Fu Panda - Characters


Po, The Dragon Warrior (VA: Jack Black)

- Po is a chubby panda who likes food. He was accidentally chosen to be the Dragon Warrior. Additionally, Po is the protagonist of Kung Fu Panda.

Master Shifu (VA: Dustin Hoffman)

- Shifu is a red panda who is the trainer of Po and the Furious Five. He is the headmaster of the Jade Palace.

Grand Master Oogway (VA: Randall Duk Kim)

- Grand Master/Master Oogway was the greatest master in kung fu history. He was the one who chose Po to be the Dragon Warrior.

The Furious Five (VA: Angelina Jolie, Lucy Liu, Jackie Chan, Seth Rogen, and David Cross)

- They are some of the most skilled warriors in China. They are homages to the Five Animals Southern styles of Chinese martial arts.

Tai Lung (VA: Ian McShane)

- Tai Lung was adopted by Master Shifu and was very powerful. He attacked the city and then was imprisoned for 20 years, until he broke out. He is the antagonist of the movie.
