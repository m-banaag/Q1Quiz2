Kung Fu Panda - Plot Summary


Plot Summary

Po, a clumsy and overweight panda who works in his father's noodle shop but dreams of becoming a kung fu master. Despite his lack of physical prowess, Po idolizes the Furious Five—Tigress, Crane, Mantis, Viper, and Monkey—who are trained by Master Shifu.

The story begins with the ancient tortoise Master Oogway seeing that Tai Lung, a powerful snow leopard and former student of Shifu, will escape from Chorh-Gom prison and seek revenge. Oogway declares that the only way to stop him is to choose the Dragon Warrior, the greatest kung fu master destined to unlock the power of the Dragon Scroll.

In a surprising twist of fate, Po is accidentally chosen as the Dragon Warrior by Oogway, much to the shock of Shifu and the Furious Five. Initially, no one believes Po is capable of becoming a kung fu master, and he struggles with training under Shifu. However, Po learns that his greatest strength is being himself. Shifu eventually realizes this and trains Po by incorporating his love for food into kung fu techniques.

Meanwhile, Tai Lung escapes from prison and comes to claim the Dragon Scroll. Po finally receives the scroll, only to discover it is blank. He realizes now that all things can be special with enough belief. Armed with this knowledge, Po faces Tai Lung in an epic battle and defeats him.

In the end, Po becomes the true Dragon Warrior, earning the respect of the Furious Five and Master Shifu.
