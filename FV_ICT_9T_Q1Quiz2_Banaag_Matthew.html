Kung Fu Panda

Po, an aspiring kung fu warrior, is "accidentally" proclaimed to be the Dragon Warrior, but he has barely any fighting skills. With Tai Lung about to escape from prison, Po must quickly learn how to harness his inner strength, master kung fu, and eat copious amounts of dumplings in order to protect the Valley of Peace from Tai Lung's wrath. Will he be able to rise to the occasion and fulfill his destiny as the Dragon Warrior?
